What We Offer

Our Services

Tailored editorial tiers to suit every phase of your writing journey. Choose one or blend them together for the ultimate polish.

I

Developmental

The Blueprint

A macro review of your entire manuscript. We audit pacing, plot consistency, plot holes, character arcs, thematic depth, and point-of-view delivery. Includes a detailed 10+ page editorial report and inline comments.

Est. Rate $0.028 / word
II

Line & Stylistic

The Polish

Sentence-by-sentence sculpting. We refine sentence structures, word choice, rhythm, tone consistency, and flow, eliminating repetitive language and filler words while amplifying the impact of your voice.

Est. Rate $0.022 / word
III

Copy & Proof

The Close Eye

The ultimate defense. We correct grammar, spelling, punctuation, typos, formatting hiccups, and consistency details (like timeline dates or eye colors) according to the Chicago Manual of Style.

Est. Rate $0.012 / word
IV

Book Formatting

The Format

Pre-publication alignment. We convert your raw document into beautiful, industry-standard digital layouts (EPUB) and print interior PDFs (KDP/IngramSpark) complete with drop caps, title styling, and margins.

Est. Rate $150 + $0.002/w
